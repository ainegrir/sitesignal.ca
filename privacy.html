2. How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver and customize permit intelligence reports
  • Process subscription payments
  • Communicate about your account, service updates, and new features
  • Improve our service quality and report relevance
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Payment processing: Stripe processes your payments and is subject to their own privacy policy.
  • Email delivery: We use third-party email services to deliver Reports. These providers process your email address solely for delivery purposes.
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Business transfer: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice to you.

4. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: We retain your information for the duration of your subscription.
  • After cancellation: Account data is retained for 90 days (for reactivation convenience), then deleted. Billing records are retained for 7 years as required by Canadian tax law.
  • Unsubscribe records: Email addresses on our suppression list are retained indefinitely to honour your opt-out request per CASL.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information, including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

Under Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA), you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw consent for non-essential data processing at any time
  • Complaint: File a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada

To exercise any right, email hello@sitesignal.ca. We respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses minimal analytics. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. Email reports may contain tracking pixels to measure open rates. You can disable image loading in your email client to opt out of this tracking.

8. Third-Party Links

Reports may contain links to municipal websites or other third-party resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is designed for business use and is not directed at individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

10. International Transfers

Your data is stored and processed in Canada. If you access the Service from outside Canada, your information may be transferred to and processed in Canada, subject to Canadian privacy laws.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email at least 14 days before taking effect. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance.
